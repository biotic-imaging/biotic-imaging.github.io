Imaging Research Data Analysis & Design

BIOTIC can help you plan and conduct imaging-related research initiatives; develop collaborations and partnerships; access and develop experimental models; prepare grant applications; recruit participants; and gather and analyze data.

Product & Algorithm Development

Our staff is highly skilled in designing, and developing analysis pipelines specific to the needs of our collaborators. Automated analysis processes developed in-house can only be found at BIOTIC.

Preclinical / Clinical Validation

With BIOTIC's pre-clinical and clinical imaging equipment, we can target: healthy function and the underpinnings of disese; disease progression and response to treatment over time; identification and validation of diagnostics and therapeutics; test efficacy and side effects; modes of delivery and dosing; and tracing drug metabolism and disposition.
