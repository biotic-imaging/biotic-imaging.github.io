Three laboratories at two BIOTIC locations

located in the IWK Health Centre and QEII Health Sciences Centre

Imaging Research Laboratory

Located at the QEII Health Sciences Centre, the NRL is equipped with the 3T MRI, EEG, and BIOTIC's main office.

Laboratory for Clinical Magnetoencephalography (MEG Lab)

Located at the IWK Health Centre, the MEG Lab houses the most advanced MEG system in Canada and is the only lab of its kind east of Montreal.

Biomedical Research Laboratory (BMRL)

Located at the IWK Health Centre, the BMRL houses a the 4T MRI and pre-clinical PET/CT systems.
